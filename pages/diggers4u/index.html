About

At Diggers4U we offer a wide range of groundwork services throughout South Warwickshire and a 20 mile radius of Snitterfield.

Coverage Area

Stratford-Upon-Avon, Warwick, Leamington Spa, Kenilworth, Coventry, Solihull, Redditch, and surrounding areas.

We carry full public liability insurance for the use of the digger.

Accessibility

Access to the working area requires a minimum width of 1m / 39 inches and a minimum height of 2.5m / 99 inches.

Please confirm these requirements are met when you enquire. If you have any doubts about accessibility, let us know. Compact digger hire is available at additional cost.
