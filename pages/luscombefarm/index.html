Caravan & Motorhome Storage

Under Cover Storage

All caravans are stored in a secure warehouse. This greatly reduces corrosion, stops parts such as the brakes from seizing up, and protects paintwork from weathering.

Friendly and Professional Staff

We specialize in high quality and reliable storage you can count on. The owners live on site and are always here to answer questions you may have.

Secure Storage Facilities

We provide storage for caravans, motorhomes, trailers, tents, boats and other leisure equipment. Items are protected by multiple site security measures and CCTV.

  • Caravans are parked inside a building and may be ready for collection 48 hours after the agreed hand-over time.
  • Arrangement by telephone generally followed up by a visit to see the site. Completed contracts and payments run from the day you actually bring your caravan, motor home, boat etc.
  • On agreement you will be given a personal number, which will be attached to your caravan.
  • Collection and delivery may only be made between 8am and 8pm unless special arrangements have previously been arranged.
  • The storage of caravans is entirely at the caravan owner's risk. It is the caravan owners' responsibility to insure the caravan and its contents against all risks, although most insurance companies look favourably on the presence of CCTV.
